Tracing Journeys

Visualizing 30 years of Global Migration and Population Change

By: Magdalena Barros

Welcome to a dynamic exploration of how populations have evolved and migrated over the past three decades. This project offers a comprehensive look at demographic trends, uncovering the forces that drive global movement and reshape societies.

Using interactive visualizations, you can trace migration flows in real-time and observe population dynamics across continents and countries. From shifts in population density to the paths of migration, this tool reveals the stories behind the data.

Designed for policymakers, researchers, and global thinkers, this project highlights the profound impact of migration on economic, social, and cultural landscapes worldwide. Dive in to explore the patterns and trends that define our interconnected world.
